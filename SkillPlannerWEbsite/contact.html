Frequently Asked Questions

Is SkillForge free to use? ▼
Yes! SkillForge is completely free for all students and self-learners. We believe quality skill planning should be accessible to everyone.
Can I track multiple skills at once? ▼
Absolutely! You can add as many skill plans as you need and track all of them simultaneously from your Progress dashboard.
Does SkillForge provide learning resources? ▼
SkillForge provides structured roadmaps and planning tools. For actual content, we recommend pairing it with platforms like Coursera, YouTube, or freeCodeCamp.
How do I earn badges and certificates? ▼
Badges are earned automatically based on milestones — like logging your first session, maintaining a streak, or completing a skill track 100%.
Can I share my progress with others? ▼
Yes! Your progress dashboard can be shared as a public profile link. Showcase your completed skills and achievements on LinkedIn or your portfolio.
